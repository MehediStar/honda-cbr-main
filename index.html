Ride A Bike Safely

Look Behind You When You Stop

If you are about to come to a stop in this situation, pull over to the side of the lane and flash your brake light several times. When you stop, watch behind you to see if a car is coming. Keep your bike in gear so you can pull away if a car approaches and it looks like it will hit you.

Never Ride Between Active Traffic

A driver could open his or her car door and you could plow right into it A car could pull out in front of you and you could crash into itA pedestrian could step out, you may not have enough time to slow down to avoid a collision. as well as remember, Drivers do not expect to be sharing lanes with motorcycles .

Never Drink And Slow Down

Drinking and riding a motorcycle is even more dangerous than drinking and driving a regular car because simply keeping the bike upright requires balance and coordination.If it is raining or there is other inclement weather, you should slow down, as you are at greater risk of crash.
